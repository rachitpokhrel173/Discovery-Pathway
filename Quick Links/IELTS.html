What is IELTS?

The International English Language Testing System (IELTS) is the most trusted test of English language proficiency, accepted by over 11,000 institutions worldwide. It assesses your ability to communicate in English for study, migration, and work.

Our training ensures you master all four modules, with strategies and practice materials designed to help you score higher and achieve your academic or migration goals.

Listening

Approximately 30 minutes of listening and additional 10 minutes to transfer answers from question booklet to answer sheet.

Reading

Exactly 60 minutes, including answer transfer time.

Writing

Exactly 60 minutes. Recommended 20 minutes for task 1 and 40 minutes for task 2.

Speaking

The test is conducted in three parts (oral interview). Part one introduces you and general topics. Part two is a cue-card for 1â€“2 minutes. Part three is a two-way discussion related to the topic in the card.

What does the score mean?

The IELTS length is about two hours (speaking separate) which is followed by the structure below.

Listening 4 sections (Academic) & 5 passages (GT) 40 minutes
