Japanese Language Tests for Student Visa to Japan

To apply for a Japanese student visa, you must pass a recognized Japanese language test such as JLPT, NAT-TEST, JLCT, J-CERT, TOP-J, or J-TEST.
Minimum Requirement: NAT, TOP-J, or J-TEST equivalent to JLPT N5.

🎓 Book Free Counseling

Why Language Tests Matter?

Japan requires international students to demonstrate Japanese proficiency for visa approval and academic success. Passing recognized tests ensures you are ready for Japanese schools, universities, and daily life in Japan.

We train students for JLPT, NAT, JLCT, J-CERT, TOP-J, and J-TEST, guiding them from beginner to advanced level for both visa requirements and long-term success in Japan.

Student Visa Requirements

Test Minimum Level Visa Advantage
JLPT N5 or higher Eligibility for language schools
NAT-TEST Equivalent to JLPT N5 Accepted for visa application
JLCT / J-CERT Equivalent to JLPT N5 Recognized by schools
TOP-J / J-TEST Equivalent to JLPT N5 Mandatory for visa approval

⚠️ You must have passed NAT, TOP-J, or J-TEST (equivalent to JLPT N5) for Japanese student visa application.
