About Us

Welcome to Discovery Pathway Pvt. Ltd

Discovery Pathway Pvt. Ltd is a leading education consultancy, headquartered in Banepa, Nepal, which was established in 2023 and is registered under the Ministry of Education, Science & Technology. We fulfill all technical and certification requirements. Our motive is to provide top-quality, reliable services to aspiring students for higher studies worldwide. We have successfully unleashed thousands of dreams for people who wish to study abroad.

Our highly skilled and experienced team provides detailed counseling to prospective students to secure admission at various universities and colleges in Europe, Australia, Japan, South Korea, UK, Canada, USA, and more. We also prepare students for IELTS and PTE exams with expert coaching by our highly trained teachers in modern high-tech classrooms.

We have strong relationships with Institutes and Universities worldwide. Our team of learned expert counselors holds not only ample experience but has also visited the respective nations' universities and acquired in-depth knowledge and training. We hold a very high admission and student visa success ratio. Trust is what we have established through people’s faith and excellent service. With our incredible success rate, Discovery Pathway is becoming one of the most renowned international education consultants in Nepal.
