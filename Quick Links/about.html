About Us

Discovery Pathway Pvt. Ltd is a leading education consultancy headquartered in Banepa, Nepal. Established in 2023, we provide top-quality guidance for students aspiring to study abroad. Our highly skilled team ensures success in admissions, visas, and test preparations.
