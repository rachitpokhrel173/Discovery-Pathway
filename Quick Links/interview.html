Interview Preparation

Visa Interview Checklist

Visa interview is an important step prior to the final confirmation of student visa approval for most countries including Australia, Canada, Germany, New Zealand, UK and USA. Your interview could fasten or delay your visa processing.

Preparatory interview questions for the Student Visa Interview:

1. Introductory Questions

  • How are you doing?
  • Have you ever been to the respective country before?

2. Questions on Intention and Motivation of Study

  • Why do you want to study in the respective country?
  • Why did you select this particular institution?
  • Why not study this course in Nepal?

3. Questions on Your Subject and Course

  • For which course are you going?
  • Why did you choose this course?

4. Questions on Funding

  • How are you going to fund your education?
  • Who is sponsoring your education?
  • Do you have enough funds?

5. Questions on Academic Qualifications

  • What is your undergraduate CGPA?
  • Can you show me your degree?

6. Questions on Achievements

  • Tell me something about your past achievements.
  • Show me any awards or certificates you have earned.

Visa Interview Tips

  • Prepare for the interview questions in advance.
  • Get a copy of all your documents with you for the interview.
  • Dress appropriately (formally).
  • Be on time for the interview.
  • Be courteous, friendly, and polite throughout the interview.
  • Be confident in your speech and answers.
  • Be brief and clear in your answers.
  • Know about your course content thoroughly.
  • Be ready for any strange questions and don’t get nervous when they’re asked.
  • Interview may take 30-40 minutes or more, so be comfortable with it.
