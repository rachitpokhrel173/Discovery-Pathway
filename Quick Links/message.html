CEO & MD Message

Amit Neupane

Chief Executive Officer

Welcome to Discovery Pathway!

As the CEO, I am honored to lead a dynamic team that is committed to transforming the future of aspiring students. We understand that choosing to study abroad is a life-changing decision—one filled with excitement, challenges, and endless possibilities. Our mission is to simplify that journey and provide students with the confidence, clarity, and tools they need to succeed globally.

We believe education is not just about academics—it’s about creating global citizens. That’s why we focus on delivering more than just services; we offer mentorship, strategy, and personalized care. Whether you aim to pursue your career in Australia, Canada, the UK, or the US, our team ensures your journey is efficient, ethical, and empowering.

Discovery Pathway is not just a consultancy—it’s a trusted companion in your success story. Thank you for choosing us to be part of your journey. Let's walk forward—together.

Suman Kumar Gautam

Managing Director

Dear Students and Parents,

It gives me great pleasure to welcome you to Discovery Pathway, a place where ambition meets opportunity. As the Managing Director, my priority is to uphold the values of integrity, transparency, and student-first service that define our organization.

We recognize that no two students are the same. Each individual has a unique dream, a personal story, and specific goals. Our job is to listen closely, guide carefully, and support consistently. From choosing the right country and university to preparing for interviews and visas, we walk with you through every step of your academic adventure abroad.

At Discovery Pathway, we strive to create a safe, supportive, and informed space for every learner. With an expert team, strong global partnerships, and up-to-date insights into international education systems, we offer a service you can trust.

Let Discovery Pathway be your compass. We are here to inspire, to guide, and to celebrate your success. The future is bright—and it's yours to claim.
