Online Application

Step-by-step guide to submitting your application online with ease.

Create an Account

Visit our website and register using your email. Set a secure password to create your account.

Fill Out Application Form

Complete the online application form with accurate personal, educational, and contact details.

Upload Documents
  • Passport copy
  • Academic transcripts
  • Financial statements
  • Recommendation letters (if applicable)
Pay Application Fee

Use the secure online payment portal to pay your application fees.

Review & Submit

Review all entered details and uploaded documents carefully before submitting your application.

Confirmation & Follow-Up

You will receive an email confirmation. Track your application status online and respond to any additional requests.

Need Assistance?

If you need help with your online application, contact Discovery Pathway for expert guidance.
