Discovery Pathway is a leading educational consultancy in Nepal, specializing in international education. We are committed to providing personalized guidance and support to students seeking to study abroad.

We specialize in:
• Personalized counseling for course and university selection
• Assistance with temporary study permits and permanent residency applications
• Support with recruitment and retention for foreign students
• End-to-end visa application services, liaising directly with immigration authorities

Our team combines local expertise with global partnerships to ensure aspiring students receive clear, tailored advice — and the hands-on support they need to study abroad confidently.

USA
Studying in the USA as an international student involves securing an
F-1 visa, demonstrating English proficiency, applying to a SEVP-approved
school, and potentially applying for OPT after graduation. Universities in the
USA offer a wide range of programs and degrees, with costs ranging from $25,000 to $45,000 per year.

Requirement:

GPA: 3.0

English Proficiency Tests: IELTS/SAT

Financial: $25,000 to $45,000 per year.
